# 💝 Cute Animated Birthday Website - PRD

## 🎯 Project Title

**Cute Animated Birthday Website for [Her Name]**

---

## 🧠 Objective

Create a **cute, animated, emotionally engaging birthday website** to wish a female friend using:

* Personalized images
* Sweet messages
* Smooth animations
* Interactive elements

**Goal:** Make her feel *special, surprised, and happy*

---

## 👩‍💻 Target Users

* **Primary:** Birthday girl
* **Secondary:** Creator (you)

---

## 🎨 Theme & Design Style

### Theme

Cute, soft, friendly (can be slightly romantic but not too much)

### Color Palette

* Pink: `#FFC0CB`
* Lavender: `#E6E6FA`
* White: `#FFFFFF`
* Soft gradients

### Fonts

* Headings: *Pacifico*, *Dancing Script*
* Body: *Poppins*, *Nunito*

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎉 1. Landing Screen

* Full-screen intro animation
* Text:

  > "Hey [Her Name] 💖, I have something special for you..."
* Button:
  **Click to Enter 🎁**
* Background:

  * Floating hearts / sparkles animation

---

### 🎂 2. Birthday Wish Section

* Typing animation:

  > "Happy Birthday [Her Name] 🎉💖"

* Subtext:

  > "You are one of the most amazing people I know…"

* Effects:

  * Balloons floating 🎈
  * Confetti animation 🎊

---

### 🖼️ 3. Photo Gallery

* Use provided images
* Layout:

  * Polaroid cards OR carousel
* Effects:

  * Hover zoom
  * Soft glow

---

### 💌 4. Message Section

Example message:

> "From all the memories to all the laughs…
> You make everything brighter ✨
> Stay happy, stay amazing 💖"

* Animation:

  * Fade-in text

---

### 🎵 5. Background Music (Optional)

* Soft instrumental music
* Toggle button:

  * ON / OFF

---

### 🎁 6. Surprise Button

Button:
**Click for a Surprise 🎁**

On click:

* Fireworks animation 🎆
* Popup message:

  > "You deserve all the happiness in the world 💖"

---

### 💖 7. Final Screen

* Text:

  > "Once Again… Happy Birthday 🎂💖"
* Falling hearts animation

---
